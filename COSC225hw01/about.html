About Me

Overview

I am currently a student at Amherst College. I have a diverse set of interests and love exploring interdisciplinary subjects. Talk to me about plants, food, or anything philosophical.

I hope to continue exploring departments outside of my own by taking at least one course in an unfamiliar department each semester.

Brief Backstory:

  1. Born in Vancouver, Canada.
  2. Family moved to California.
  3. Arrived at Amherst with no game plan and randomly threw intro cs onto my schedule. I had fun and decided to keep taking courses in the department.
